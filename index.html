Largest and First

HIT is The Largest and First Self Financed Residential Engineering Institute in West Bengal, established in 1996. HIT, spreading across 37 acres of land, is situated at Haldia, West Bengal, an industrial hub in eastern India. The Institute is dedicated to the objectives of creating highly trained professional manpower in various disciplines of engineering, technology, social and management sciences.

A KNOWLEDGE HUB WITH TECHNO-PEDANTIC LOOM

Why Choose Us

The Institute has full time faculty strength of 246, out of which 90 faculty members are having PhD. HIT has dedicated team of teachers who are committed to igniting the spark of creativity and inspiring a quest for knowledge in the minds of students.

 Well Equipped and state-of-the-art laboratories in all engineering & management Departments

 Campus wide Wi-Fi facility with 100 Mbps high speed leased line

 Several central workshops housed with latest equipment

 Class rooms and seminar halls are equipped with Audio Visual facilities

The picturesque site and blossoming green environment makes the campus, free from pollution. The campus is spread over 37 acres of lush green land. We have a well ventilated building structure with safe and hygienic indoors and outdoors

The Institute has state-of-the-art research facilities for technical, scientific and industrial research. Various Departments of the institute are engaged in different research projects sponsored by the DST, AICTE, UGC and many other Govt. agencies

The library (2935 sq. m area) has a rich collection of 114259 books, 161 print journals, 10 databases of e-resource journals (Science direct, J-GATE, IEEE, ASTM, ASCE, Springer, etc.) covering about 34114 e-journals.
